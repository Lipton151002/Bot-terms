# Privacy Policy for Killa-Bot

Last Updated: 28.11.2025

---

## 1. Overview

Killa-Bot is a private, non-public bot.  
It does not collect data for advertising, analytics, or commercial usage.

Only data required for bot functionality is processed.

---

## 2. Data Collected

Depending on features, the bot may process the following information:

- User IDs
- Server IDs
- Roles and permissions
- Commands sent to the bot
- Message content (ONLY if required for anti-abuse, logging, moderation, or bot features)

The bot does **NOT** collect personal identifiable information (PII) such as name, phone number, email, address, or payment information.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

All stored data (if any) is kept privately and securely by the bot owner.  
Access is restricted and never shared with third parties.

Any attempt to:

- extract stored data,
- access private logs,
- compromise authentication tokens,
- manipulate or reverse-engineer the bot

is considered unauthorized and may violate security and legal regulations.

---

## 4. Data Removal Requests

The bot owner or an authorized server administrator may request removal of stored data related to:

- a user,
- a Discord server,
- or bot configuration.

Upon request, the data will be permanently deleted.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The bot communicates only with the **official Discord API**.  
No external tracking, analytics services, or third-party data processors are used.

---

## 6. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated at any time.  
Continued use of the bot after changes means acceptance of the updated policy.
